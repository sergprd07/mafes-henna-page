Getting the Darkest Stain

How to get a deeper, longer-lasting stain.

  • Arrive with clean, product-free skin. Avoid lotions, oils, sunscreen, and heavy fragrances on the area to be hennaed.
  • Wear loose clothing or sleeves that won’t rub against the fresh paste while it dries.
  • For bridal henna, schedule your appointment 1–3 days before your event to allow the stain to fully develop and reach its deepest color.
  • Leave the paste on for at least 4 hours, or longer whenever possible.
  • Gently scrape off the dried paste and avoid washing the area right away.
  • Keep the stain warm, and avoid swimming, exfoliating, or using harsh soaps during the first 24 hours.
