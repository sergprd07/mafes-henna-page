How to get a deeper, longer-lasting stain.

  • Arrive with clean skin and no lotion, oil, sunscreen, or heavy fragrance on the area.
  • Wear sleeves or outfits that will not rub the fresh paste.
  • Book bridal henna one to three days before the main event for the richest color.
  • Keep the paste on for at least four hours, longer when possible.
  • Scrape paste off gently; avoid washing the area immediately.
  • Keep the stain warm and avoid pools, exfoliation, and heavy soaps for the first day.
  • Send guest count, event length, parking details, and whether designs should be simple or detailed.
  • For high guest counts, choose a design menu so everyone gets time with the artist.
  • Set up one table, two chairs, good light, and a small waiting area.
